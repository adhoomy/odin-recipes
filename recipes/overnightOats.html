Overnight Oats

Description

Overnight oats is an easy way to preprare a morning breakfast the previous night. All you need is a mug, spoon, and the following ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Protein Powder (optional)
  • Any Fruits and Almonds/Nuts of Your Liking (optional)

Steps

  1. Pour 1/2 cup of oats into a mug.
  2. Add 1/2 cup of oats.
  3. 2/3 cup of greek yogurt.
  4. Add protein powder (optional).
  5. Mix it together.
  6. Seal and keep it in the fridge overnight.
  7. The next morning, add any fruits and almonds/nuts of your liking.
  8. Enjoy!
